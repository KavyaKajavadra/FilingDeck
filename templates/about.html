Why We Started FilingDeck

We saw a problem that millions of small businesses in India face: compliance is confusing, expensive, and time-consuming. Traditional CA firms charge premium rates, respond slowly, and offer little transparency.

We asked ourselves — what if compliance could be as simple as a WhatsApp message? What if a small business owner could get GST filed for ₹999/month instead of hefty traditional fees? What if they never missed a deadline again because someone was actually tracking it for them?

That's why we built FilingDeck. Our team uniquely combines CA expertise (taxation, GST, TDS), financial market insight, and technology — all under one roof. This combination doesn't exist at traditional firms.

We're young, we're tech-savvy, and we're hungry to prove that compliance doesn't have to be painful. Based in Mumbai and Thane, we serve businesses across Maharashtra and beyond.
